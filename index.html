2. Data Collection

FootINet does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. Specifically:

  • No registration or account is required
  • No names, emails, or phone numbers are collected
  • No device identifiers are collected (IMEI, Android ID, Advertising ID)
  • No geolocation data is collected from the device
  • No cookies are used
  • No analytics or crash reporting systems are integrated

3. External Services

The app connects to the following external services exclusively in a read-only capacity to retrieve public information. No personal data is transmitted to these services:

  • RSS Feeds — sports news aggregation from public sources
  • GitHub — reading a cached JSON file with aggregated news
  • OpenStreetMap (Nominatim) — geocoding locations for map markers
  • API-Football (RapidAPI) — results, standings, and statistics
  • Yahoo Finance / Polygon.io — financial and stock market data
  • Polymarket — odds and predictive markets
  • OpenWeatherMap — stadium weather data

4. Locally Stored Data

The app saves exclusively the following data on your device:

  • News cache: aggregated RSS data to reduce network requests
  • Geocoding cache: coordinates of previously searched locations
  • User preferences: filters and custom settings

All local data is permanently deleted upon uninstalling the app.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies, web beacons, browser fingerprinting, or any other tracking technologies.

6. External Links

The app may display links to external articles and websites. Once you follow a link, the privacy policy of the destination website applies. FootINet is not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from minors.

In accordance with EU Regulation 2016/679, users have the right to:

  • Request access to their data

Since the app does not collect any personal data, these rights are inherently satisfied. For any inquiries, please contact the Data Controller at the provided email address.
